About

I am the lead developer at LEFT. We created a web app for trip coordinators and a mobile app for their travelers to improve the trip and trip planning experience. Both apps are based on the MERN stack. Before that I worked on the DevOps team at Cornerstone OnDemand. My team and I handled deployments, created dashboards, maintained delivery pipelines, and provided tools to the dev teams.
